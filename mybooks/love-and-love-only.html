❤️ Love and Love Only

Love that will never break you… because He was broken for you.

What if everything you called love… was only a temporary feeling?

You trusted.

You cared.

You gave your heart fully.

But still… it broke.

Maybe it was rejection.

Maybe it was silence.

Maybe it was someone who slowly changed.

Was that really love?

Or was it something else… something that only looked like love but left you empty inside?

You are not alone.

Millions are searching for love. But many are hurting because they were never shown what real love looks like.

We confuse attention with love.

We confuse attachment with love.

We confuse feelings with love.

And that confusion… leads to pain.

This book will change how you see love.

Love and Love Only is not just about relationships. It is about understanding yourself, healing from within, and discovering a love that does not change.

Not temporary.

Not conditional.

Not confusing.

But real. Stable. Lasting.

What makes this book different?

✅ Helps you understand why love often hurts

✅ Guides you through emotional healing

✅ Teaches you to stop losing yourself in relationships

✅ Shows the difference between attention and true love

✅ Leads you toward a deeper, lasting foundation

This is where healing begins.

Healing is not about forgetting what happened. It is about understanding it, growing through it, and not losing yourself in the process.

A love that never changes

There is a kind of love that does not leave… does not break… does not fail.

A love that remains — even when everything else doesn’t. This book gently points you toward that love.
